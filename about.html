Our Story

Developed from an unexpected demand for dynamic aerial footage, and elevated with the principles of quality & ease, DroneMVP seamlessly connects content seekers with expert drone pilots to create captivating images together.

A few years ago, we went to the Interdrone Festival in Las Vegas to advertise the LA Drone Festival, a drone footage competition judged by Eric Duping (Dronestagram), Yofray Ray (I’mShmacked & College Weekly) & Steve Harris (Electric Gaming). The competition sought to find the top 3 pilots. With over 500 submissions from around the world, we were not only able to see the beauty of aerial cinematography, but also the capacity drone technology could play in producing one-of-a-kind content. This discovery led us to share the submitted footage on the competitions social media channels. An overwhelming response from viewers surfaced, asking us who produced the footage and where they could find them. This inspired us to create DroneMVP, a network of drone pilots that sought to seamlessly connect people with the perfect pilot to create stunning content. With the expertise of our pilots and the vision of our creative directors, we developed an innovative process to connect, capture and deliver the highest quality of footage.

DroneMVP aims to help drone pilots make money & help content seekers save money. Our mission is to deliver each of our clients with the best possible visuals for their desired projects. We work with a range of industries from real estate, film, live events, commercials and virtual reality to elevate an ordinary image to an extraordinary network.
